Bell Track

A simple workout log designed for kettlebell and EMOM-style training. Log your sets quickly, stay consistent, and see your progress over time — without distractions or complexity.

Track what matters

  • Exercise or set name
  • Weight (single or double)
  • Rounds or reps
  • Notes
  • Date

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Bell Track is a simple workout logging app. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your data.

Information We Collect

  • Email address when you create an account
  • Workout entries you choose to log in the app

This data is used only to provide app functionality such as securely storing your account and workouts. Data is stored using Firebase Authentication and Firebase Firestore.

What We Don't Do

  • We do NOT sell your data
  • We do NOT share your data with advertisers
  • We do NOT track you across other apps or websites

Third-Party Services

We use Firebase to handle authentication and cloud storage. You can read Google’s Firebase privacy policy here:
https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

Your Data Rights

You may request account deletion at any time from within the app. This permanently removes your account and workout history.
